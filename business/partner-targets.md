# Partner Targets

## Integration Roadmap

1. **Steam API**
   - **Rationale**: Steam is the largest digital distribution platform for PC games, offering a vast user base and extensive game library.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 100,000 requests per day.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium (M).
   - **Value-add**: Enables real-time game crash detection and reporting for Steam users.

2. **Epic Games Store API**
   - **Rationale**: Epic Games Store is a significant competitor to Steam, with a growing user base.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 100,000 requests per day.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium (M).
   - **Value-add**: Provides crash detection and reporting for Epic Games Store users.

3. **Origin API**
   - **Rationale**: Origin is another major digital distribution platform, owned by EA.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 100,000 requests per day.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium (M).
   - **Value-add**: Offers crash detection and reporting for Origin users.

4. **Battle.net API**
   - **Rationale**: Battle.net is the digital distribution platform for Blizzard games, which have a dedicated and loyal fan base.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 100,000 requests per day.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium (M).
   - **Value-add**: Enables crash detection and reporting for Blizzard game users.

5. **GOG Galaxy API**
   - **Rationale**: GOG Galaxy is a digital distribution platform that focuses on DRM-free games.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 100,000 requests per day.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium (M).
   - **Value-add**: Provides crash detection and reporting for GOG Galaxy users.

6. **Twitch API**
   - **Rationale**: Twitch is a popular streaming platform where gamers often encounter crashes during live streams.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 100,000 requests per day.
   - **Integration effort**: Large (L).
   - **Value-add**: Enables real-time crash detection and reporting during live streams.

7. **Discord API**
   - **Rationale**: Discord is a popular communication platform used by gamers to coordinate and discuss games.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 100,000 requests per day.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium (M).
   - **Value-add**: Provides a platform for gamers to report crashes and receive support.

8. **NVIDIA GeForce Experience API**
   - **Rationale**: NVIDIA GeForce Experience is a popular software suite for NVIDIA GPU users.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 100,000 requests per day.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium (M).
   - **Value-add**: Enables real-time system stability monitoring and crash prevention for NVIDIA GPU users.
