# Marketing Plan
## Positioning
Game Guardian: AI-powered system stability monitoring and crash prevention for PC gamers.
Alternative Positioning Options:
1. **Proactive System Shield**: Emphasizing protection against system crashes.
2. **Gaming Performance Optimizer**: Focusing on enhancing gaming performance.
3. **Crash Prevention Specialist**: Highlighting the tool's ability to prevent hard resets.

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
1. **Ethan "The Enthusiast"**: Daily job - Streaming gaming content, Budget - $100/month.
2. **Liam "The Competitive Gamer"**: Daily job - Participating in esports tournaments, Budget - $200/month.
3. **Noah "The Avid Gamer"**: Daily job - Playing games for leisure, Budget - $50/month.

## Channels
Top 3 Channels with Estimated CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):
1. **Social Media (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram)**: CAC - $15/user.
2. **Influencer Marketing (Gaming Influencers)**: CAC - $30/user.
3. **Google Ads (Targeted Gaming Keywords)**: CAC - $20/user.

## Content Cadence
Week-by-Week Content Plan:
- Week 1-2: Introduction to Game Guardian, problem statement, and solution overview.
- Week 3-4: Features and benefits, technical details, and user testimonials.
- Week 5-6: Gaming performance optimization tips, system stability best practices.
- Week 7-8: Success stories, case studies, and community engagement.
- Week 9-10: Advanced features, upcoming updates, and loyalty programs.
- Week 11-12: User-generated content campaign, referral programs.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30 (30 days before launch)**: Finalize marketing materials, begin social media teaser campaign.
- **D-0 (Launch Day)**: Official launch, press release, influencer partnerships activation.
- **D+30 (30 days after launch)**: Analyze user feedback, optimize marketing channels.
- **D+90 (90 days after launch)**: Review success metrics, plan for future updates and expansions.

## Success Metrics
- **DAU (Daily Active Users)**: Target 10,000 DAU by D+90.
- **$MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)**: Target $100,000 MRR by D+90.
