# Customer Journey Map
## Overview
The customer journey map for Game Guardian outlines the phases a potential customer goes through from initial awareness to expansion of the product. This map highlights trigger events, friction points, user emotions, opportunities to delight, and key metrics for each phase.

### Aware
* **Trigger Event**: Experience of a hard reset or system crash during a gaming session, leading to frustration and loss of progress.
* **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about system stability monitoring tools, difficulty in finding effective solutions.
* **User Emotions**: Frustration, disappointment, and a sense of helplessness.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Introduction to Game Guardian through social media, gaming forums, or influencer reviews, highlighting its AI-powered predictive analytics and crash prevention capabilities.
* **Metric**: Number of unique visitors to the Game Guardian website or social media channels.

### Consider
* **Trigger Event**: Research into system stability and crash prevention tools after experiencing a significant loss due to a hard reset.
* **Friction Points**: Skepticism about the effectiveness of AI-powered tools, concerns about system compatibility and resource usage.
* **User Emotions**: Curiosity, cautious optimism, and a desire for a reliable solution.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Detailed product reviews, testimonials from satisfied users, and a free trial or demo version of Game Guardian to alleviate concerns.
* **Metric**: Number of downloads of the free trial version, engagement with product demos or tutorials.

### Try
* **Trigger Event**: Decision to try Game Guardian based on positive reviews, recommendations, or the outcome of the free trial.
* **Friction Points**: Difficulty in installing or configuring the software, initial doubts about its effectiveness.
* **User Emotions**: Hope, anticipation, and a critical mindset to evaluate the product's performance.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Seamless installation process, intuitive interface, and immediate benefits such as reduced crashes or improved system stability.
* **Metric**: Activation rate of the full version of Game Guardian, user retention rate after the first week of use.

### Adopt
* **Trigger Event**: Successful prevention of hard resets or significant improvement in gaming session stability, leading to satisfaction with Game Guardian.
* **Friction Points**: None significant, as the product has proven its value.
* **User Emotions**: Satisfaction, relief, and loyalty towards the brand.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Regular software updates with new features, premium support, and community engagement to foster a sense of belonging.
* **Metric**: Customer satisfaction ratings, net promoter score (NPS), and subscription renewal rates.

### Expand
* **Trigger Event**: Long-term satisfaction with Game Guardian, leading to a willingness to explore additional features or recommend the product to others.
* **Friction Points**: Potential desire for more advanced features or integration with other gaming tools.
* **User Emotions**: Loyalty, advocacy, and a continued desire for innovation and support.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer of premium services, early access to new features, or involvement in beta testing and community events.
* **Metric**: Revenue growth from upselling or cross-selling, increase in user-generated content or community engagement, and expansion of the user base through word-of-mouth referrals.

By understanding and addressing the needs and emotions of potential customers at each phase of the customer journey, Game Guardian can effectively attract, retain, and expand its user base, ultimately achieving its goal of providing a stable and enjoyable gaming experience.
