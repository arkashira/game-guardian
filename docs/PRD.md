# Product Requirements Document
## Game Guardian
### Overview

Game Guardian is an AI-powered system stability monitoring and crash prevention tool designed for PC gamers. The primary goal of this product is to detect and prevent hard resets during gaming sessions, ensuring a seamless and uninterrupted gaming experience.

### Problem Statement

PC gamers often experience frustrating hard resets during intense gaming sessions, resulting in lost progress, wasted time, and a negative gaming experience. Current solutions rely on manual monitoring and lack predictive capabilities, failing to prevent crashes effectively.

### Target Users

* Demographics: PC gamers aged 18-45
* Psychographics: Individuals who value a seamless gaming experience and are willing to invest in tools to enhance their gaming performance
* User personas:
	+ Casual gamers: Play games for relaxation and entertainment
	+ Competitive gamers: Participate in esports tournaments and require optimal system performance
	+ Gaming enthusiasts: Experiment with various games and settings, seeking to optimize their gaming experience

### Goals

1. **Crash Prevention**: Detect and prevent hard resets during gaming sessions using AI-powered predictive analytics
2. **System Stability**: Monitor and maintain system stability, ensuring a smooth gaming experience
3. **User Satisfaction**: Provide a user-friendly interface and timely alerts to ensure gamers can take proactive measures to prevent crashes

### Key Features (Prioritized)

1. **AI-powered Predictive Analytics** (High Priority)
	* Utilize machine learning algorithms to analyze system metrics and predict potential crashes
	* Integrate with popular gaming platforms to collect relevant data
2. **Real-time System Monitoring** (High Priority)
	* Monitor system resources (CPU, GPU, RAM, etc.) and detect anomalies
	* Provide real-time alerts and notifications to users
3. **Automatic Crash Prevention** (Medium Priority)
	* Implement automated measures to prevent crashes, such as adjusting system settings or terminating resource-intensive processes
4. **User Alerts and Notifications** (Medium Priority)
	* Provide customizable alerts and notifications to inform users of potential crashes or system instability
5. **Gaming Session Analytics** (Low Priority)
	* Offer insights into gaming session performance, including crash frequency, system resource usage, and other relevant metrics

### Success Metrics

1. **Crash Rate Reduction**: Measure the percentage decrease in hard resets during gaming sessions
2. **User Satisfaction**: Track user feedback and ratings to ensure the product meets their expectations
3. **System Stability**: Monitor system stability and performance metrics to ensure the product is effective in maintaining a smooth gaming experience

### Scope

* Develop an AI-powered predictive analytics engine to detect and prevent hard resets
* Design a user-friendly interface for real-time system monitoring and alerts
* Integrate with popular gaming platforms to collect relevant data
* Implement automated crash prevention measures

### Out-of-Scope

* Developing a full-fledged gaming platform or launcher
* Providing direct technical support for individual games or system configurations
* Creating a mobile or console version of the product

### Assumptions and Dependencies

* Access to relevant system metrics and gaming platform APIs
* Availability of sufficient computational resources for AI-powered predictive analytics
* User willingness to install and configure the product

### Timeline and Milestones

* Research and development: 6 weeks
* Alpha release: 3 weeks
* Beta release: 6 weeks
* Full release: 12 weeks

### Budget and Resources

* Personnel: 2-3 developers, 1 AI/ML engineer, 1 UX designer
* Infrastructure: Cloud-based servers for data processing and storage
* Budget: $200,000 - $300,000 (dependent on personnel and infrastructure costs)
